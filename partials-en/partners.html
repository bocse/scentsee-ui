ScentSee for Business

ScentSee is an easy-to-integrate solution for managing fragrance recommendation in your online shop or brick&mortar store. Moreover, the ScentSee API can be called by your existing business application at any step of the purchase process, from discovery to ordering to fulfillment. Scentsee is available as a dedicated or whitelabel solution, for online and in-store experiences.
